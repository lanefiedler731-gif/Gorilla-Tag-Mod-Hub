## Included Mods

The **Christmas Mod Drop** features a core set of utilities designed to enhance your gameplay and keep you safe.

### Anti-Cheat (Public Beta) - **Priority**
Our flagship mod for this release. A robust system designed to protect modded players.
- **Report Blocking:** Prevents `GorillaNot` and Scoreboard reports from reaching the server.
- **RPC Spoofing:** Hides modded signatures from network logs.
- **Stealth Hooks:** Utilizes advanced hooking techniques to remain undetected.
- **Activity Log:** Real-time feedback on blocked reports and network events.

### üèÉ WalkSim Modern
The evolution of WalkSim Reborn, optimized for the latest Steam version.
- **Controls:** WASD movement and Mouse camera controls.
- **Menu:** Instant `TAB` panel for speed, camera, and locomotion tweaks.
- **Bypass:** Works perfectly in Red Lobbies.

### Path Recorder (`F2`)
Record complex movement sequences and play them back perfectly. Perfect for cinematic shots or training.

### Player Customizer (`F3`)
Quick-access menu to change your gorilla's colors and cosmetics on the fly without returning to the stump.

### Player Info Mod (`F5`)
View detailed statistics and information about other players in your current lobby.

### Sound Board
Map custom `.mp3` files to your numpad for instant audio triggering in-game.

### Red Lobbies
A utility that ensures you can always find a match, allowing you to connect with other modded/banned players.
